Italian Candied Chestnut

This is a nice sweet that will bring your meal to a perfect ending.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds of shelled fresh chestnuts
  • 2 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 pounds of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla

Steps

  1. Place Chestnut in pan with water just enough to cover them. Bring to boil and cook chestnut for 10 minutes
  2. strain the chestnuts
  3. rub the chestnuts with clean dishtowel in order to remove the skin
  4. In seperate pan put the 2 1/2 cups of water, granulated sugar, and vanilla to a boil while stirring
  5. Cook and mix for 5 minutes
  6. Add chestnuts to mixture and boil. Boil for 10 minutes
  7. Pour chestnuts into large loose container and allow the chestnuts to soak for 12 to 18 hours in the syrup.
  8. After soak put chestnuts in pan and boil for 2 minutes
  9. Take out and let rest. Repeat the boiling for 2 minutes and rest for a total of 3 times
  10. Bake chestnuts for 250 F degrees on parchment paper
  11. Let cool for an hour and then enjoy
